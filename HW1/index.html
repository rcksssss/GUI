What Do The Pokémon Symbols Mean?

In the Pokémon world, each type has its own strengths and weaknesses. These type symbols represent elemental categories such as Fire, Water, Grass, and Electric. The table below shows some of the most common types, what they are strong against, and what they are weak against. Types add depth to battles by encouraging strategy and team variety. Most trainers often cover weaknesses with various Pokémon lineups, making matchups more engaging and rewarding for both casual and competitive play.

Type Symbol Strong Against Weak Against
Fire Grass, Bug, Ice, Steel Water, Rock, Ground
Water Fire, Rock, Ground Electric, Grass
Grass Water, Rock, Ground Fire, Bug, Ice, Flying
Electric Water, Flying Ground

Famous Pokémon of These Types

Below are some of the most famous Pokémon that represent these elemental types. Each one has become iconic thanks to their appearances in games, shows, and movies:

  • Fire – Charizard
  • Water – Squirtle
  • Grass – Bulbasaur
  • Electric – Pikachu
